Contrast

Contrast deals with highlighting the differences between design elements. For example here in this screenshot it helps draw attention to specific areas and put the viewer in a state of being interested in what they are viewing. Using contrasting colors, fonts, or sizes to make important elements stand out, like the image, the words, and also the background not interfering with the entire view.

Alignment

Alignment also does with the arranging of elements along a common line or edge. It brings a sense of order and agrrement to a design. Good alignment helps create a visually appealing and balanced layout, like in the example above the images and the words explaining them are written in a well aligned manner, making it possible for the viewer to see it all clearly and orderly, as they are aligned along the left side flowing down.

White Space and Clean Design

White space (or negative space) is the empty space between design elements. It helps reduce the struggle an individual gets while seeing through closely fitted stuffs and allows the viewer to focus on the essential elements. Clean design emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and forsakes the idea of wanting to fill all the space with somethings that don't matter.
